Spectral Throw
Attack, Projectile
Level: 20 (Max)
Cost: 8 Mana
Attack Speed: 110% of base
Attack Damage: 125% of base
Effectiveness of Added Damage: 125%
Quality: +20%
Requires Level 70, 68 Str, 98 Dex
Throws a spectral copy of your melee weapon. It flies out and then returns to you, in a spinning attack that damages enemies in its path.
Projectiles that have Pierced deal 10% more Damage
Place into an item socket of the right colour to gain this skill. Right click to remove from a socket.
