Raise Spectre
Spell, Minion
Level: 20 (Max)
Cost: 28 Mana
Cast Time: 0.85 sec
Quality: +20%
Requires Level 70, 155 Int
Raises a spectral version of a defeated foe as a minion to fight for you in battle.
Maximum 2 Raised Spectres
Minions have +50% to all Elemental Resistances
55% more Minion Movement Speed
Minion Movement Speed is Capped
Raised Spectres are Level 70
Minions have 776 additional Accuracy Rating
Place into an item socket of the right colour to gain this skill. Right click to remove from a socket.
