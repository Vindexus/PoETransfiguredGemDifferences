Blade Trap
Trap, Attack, AoE
Level: 20 (Max)
Cost: 20 Mana
Attack Damage: 96% of base
Effectiveness of Added Damage: 96%
Quality: +20%
Requires Level 70, 155 Dex
Throws a trap which, once triggered, swings two copies of your equipped Dagger, Claw or One Handed Sword around it in circles, each repeatedly damaging enemies it spins through.
Trap lasts 5 seconds
Rotates 5 times
25% increased rotation speed if Dual Wielding
Rotates +1 times if Dual Wielding
Base radius is 2.2 metres
Place into an item socket of the right colour to gain this skill. Right click to remove from a socket.
