Eye of Winter of Transience
Spell, Projectile, Cold
Level: 20 (Max)
Cost: 26 Mana
Cast Time: 0.80 sec
Critical Strike Chance: 6.00%
Quality: +20%
Requires Level 70, 155 Int
Fires a single eye projectile which releases a number shard projectiles in a spiral before dissipating. The shards will damage enemies they impact, dealing cold damage. The eye cannot damage enemies.
Deals 458 to 687 Cold Damage
Modifiers to number of Projectiles only apply to the Shards
Fires 12 Shard Projectiles in a Spiral while flying, then ends
50% more Projectile Speed
Projectiles continuously gain Damage, up to 15% more Damage by the time they dissipate
Place into an item socket of the right colour to gain this skill. Right click to remove from a socket.
