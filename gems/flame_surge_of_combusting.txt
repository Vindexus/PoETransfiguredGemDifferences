Flame Surge
Spell, AoE, Fire, Duration
Level: 20 (Max)
Cost: 16 Mana
Cast Time: 0.50 sec
Critical Strike Chance: 6.00%
Effectiveness of Added Damage: 190%
Quality: +20%
Requires Level 70, 155 Int
Strikes enemies in front of you with a surge of flame. Burning enemies are dealt more damage. If you hit an ignited enemy, will create burning ground under them. Your damage modifiers don't apply to this burning ground.
Base duration is 4.00 seconds
Deals 832 to 1247 Fire Damage
108% more Damage with Hits against Burning enemies
Cannot Ignite
+9 to Area of Effect length
Hits on Ignited Enemies create Burning Ground, no more than once every 2 seconds
Burning Ground deals 25% of Fire Damage per second of Ignite affecting Enemy
Place into an item socket of the right colour to gain this skill. Right click to remove from a socket.
