Detonate Dead
Spell, AoE, Fire
Level: 20 (Max)
Cost: 20 Mana
Cast Time: 0.60 sec
Critical Strike Chance: 6.00%
Effectiveness of Added Damage: 160%
Quality: +20%
Requires Level 70, 98 Dex, 68 Int
Targets a corpse, and deals spell damage to enemies around the corpse, as well as causing the corpse to explode, dealing fire damage. The explosion is not affected by modifiers to spell damage and cannot be reflected.
Deals 798 to 1197 Fire Damage
Explosion deals base Fire Damage equal to 7.9% of the corpse's Maximum Life
Base radius is 2.6 metres
15% chance to detonate an additional corpse
Place into an item socket of the right colour to gain this skill. Right click to remove from a socket.
