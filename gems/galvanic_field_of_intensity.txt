Galvanic Field
Spell, AoE, Duration, Lightning, Chaining, Orb
Level: 20 (Max)
Cost: 18 Mana
Cast Time: 0.50 sec
Critical Strike Chance: 6.00%
Quality: +20%
Requires Level 70, 155 Int
Applies a buff boosting chance to shock. When you shock an enemy while you have this buff, creates a spherical field of energy attached to the shocked enemy for a duration, which will damage it and other nearby enemies with beams of lightning. The strength of the field depends on the magnitude of shock affecting the enemy when it is created.
Deals 56 to 1067 Lightning Damage
Cannot inflict Shock
Base duration is 6.00 seconds
Fires a beam every 0.10 seconds
Can target each enemy only once every 0.50 seconds
Field gains 15% more Damage with Hits per 5% Shock Effect on Enemy when created
Field gains +0.1 metres to radius per 10% Shock Effect on Enemy when created
Maximum 1 Field
Buff Grants 40% chance to Shock enemies
Place into an item socket of the right colour to gain this skill. Right click to remove from a socket.
